Earthships & Aquaponis!?

Yes, earthship!, not many people are familiar with the modern "earthship" but building one from scratch is a huge dream for me. An earthship is a home made out of natural and/or reciclyed materials, most earthships are of grid meaning they dont need energy or water from private or govenrment sorces. I love earthships, i love the idea of building something from the ground up and not only that you're also saving the earth by getting rid of tires and other materials that pollute our land and waters.
